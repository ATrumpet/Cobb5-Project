ABOUT US

Welcome to Tech Innovators Cafe, an authentic International restaurant with a modern twist! Restauranteur, Tech Innovators Team, has brought the city of Atlanta its passion for Internatioal food to the next level at our restuarant. The restaurant boasts a contemporary ambiance mixed in with global contemporary cuisine. Our worldly trained Chef's bring experience, knowledge, and creativity to your dining experience at Tech Innovators Cafe. Our menu is full of flavors that can entice any palate. It's also offers a unique list of cocktails inspired by spice-infused liqueur. The ambiance, from our sultry & chic lounge to the serene dining hall, will make this a dining experience you won't forget.



Come dine with us!

Fresh food prep

At Tech Innovators Cafe we take a "cook less, more often" approach. By studying smart, lean manufacturing principles used in other industries, we have created new ways to serve our guests the freshest food possible while reducing food waste.


About Catering

Our team has over 75 years of experience in all styles of International food. We offer a new twist on traditional recipes and time honored favorites, while keeping with our global contemporary cuisine. Let us entice your taste buds!
